# AI and Cloud Services Used

## Language Model Usage
The chatbot uses a cloud-hosted large language model provided through the AI agent platform. The model understands user input and generates relevant responses related to nutrition and health.

---

## AI Platform Role
- Handles natural language understanding
- Generates responses without manual prompt engineering
- Manages scalability and availability

---

## Why No Custom Model or RAG?
For this assignment, the focus is on clarity and correctness rather than advanced experimentation. The chosen approach demonstrates practical AI usage without unnecessary complexity.
